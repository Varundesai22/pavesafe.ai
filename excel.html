Exporting Data to Excel

You can export the detected road defect data to an Excel (.xlsx) file. This file will contain a detailed record of the identified defects, their severity, and location.

Information Included:

  • Defect Type: The category of the detected road defect (e.g., pothole, crack).
  • Severity Score: A numerical value indicating the severity of the defect.
  • Latitude: The latitude coordinate of the defect's location.
  • Longitude: The longitude coordinate of the defect's location.
  • Detection Timestamp: The date and time when the defect was detected.

Note: Click the "Generate Road Safety Audit Report" button on the homepage to trigger the export. This functionality will be implemented in the backend to generate and download the Excel file.
